The Skill Olympics, called the WorldSkills Competitions, are the gold standard of skills excellence. It is the largest skills competition in the world, organized once every two years in one of the member countries. More than 1400+ contestants below the age of 22, compete for gold, silver and bronze medals, in over 62 skills.
